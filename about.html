About Thomson's Auto Body Repair

Your trusted partner for professional vehicle restoration

Our Story

Thomson's Auto Body Repair has been serving the Ladybrand community for over two decades. We started with a simple mission: to provide honest, high-quality vehicle repair services that customers can trust. Today, we've grown into one of the region's most respected auto body repair shops.

Our commitment to excellence, combined with our team's expertise and dedication, has made us the go-to choice for vehicle owners and insurance companies alike. We take pride in every repair we undertake, ensuring that your vehicle is restored to its original condition.

Our Values

Quality

We never compromise on quality. Every repair is performed to the highest standards using premium materials and proven techniques.

Integrity

Honest communication and transparent pricing are the foundation of our relationships with customers and insurance partners.

Expertise

Our team consists of certified technicians with extensive training and real-world experience in all aspects of vehicle repair.

Customer Service

We treat every customer with respect and provide personalized attention throughout the repair process.

Our Team

Our experienced team of technicians, painters, and support staff are dedicated to delivering exceptional results. With combined experience spanning decades, we have the expertise to handle any vehicle repair challenge.
