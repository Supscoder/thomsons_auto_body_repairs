Our Services

Comprehensive vehicle repair and restoration solutions

What We Offer

We provide a complete range of auto body repair services to restore your vehicle to its original condition. Whether you've been in an accident or need cosmetic improvements, our expert team is ready to help.

Panel Beating

Expert dent removal and body panel restoration. We use specialized equipment to carefully reshape damaged panels without compromising structural integrity.

Spray Painting

Professional paint services with advanced color matching technology. Our painters ensure seamless finishes that match your vehicle's original paint.

Collision Repair

Complete collision damage assessment and repair. We work with insurance companies and use manufacturer specifications for all repairs.

Welding Services

Structural welding and reinforcement for comprehensive vehicle restoration. Our welders are certified and experienced in all welding techniques.

Glass Replacement

Professional windscreen and window replacement with OEM-quality materials. We ensure proper installation and sealing for safety.

Customization

Custom body modifications and aesthetic enhancements. From minor tweaks to major transformations, we bring your vision to life.

Bumper Repair

Bumper repair and replacement services. We handle plastic and metal bumpers with precision and care.

Frame Straightening

Advanced frame alignment and straightening using state-of-the-art equipment. Ensures your vehicle handles properly after collision damage.

Insurance Claims

We work directly with insurance companies to streamline your claims process and handle all paperwork.
